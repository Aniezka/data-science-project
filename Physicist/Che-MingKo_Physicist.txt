Che-Ming Ko (柯治明; born 1943) is a Taiwanese physicist, focusing in nuclear theory, currently University Distinguished Professor  at Texas A&M University and an Elected Fellow of the American Physical Society in 1994. Ko received a BS degree from Tunghai University in Taiwan. Ko received a MS degree from McMaster University in Canada. Ko received his Ph.D. in 1973 from State University of New York at Stony Brook, under dissertation advisor Gerald E. Brown.
