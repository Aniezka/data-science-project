Li-Chyong Chen (Chinese: 林麗瓊) is a Taiwanese physicist. Chen completed her bachelor's of science degree in physics at National Taiwan University between 1977 and 1981. She then attended Harvard University from 1983 to 1989, where she earned a doctorate in applied physics. Chen returned to Taiwan in 1994, and is a professor of physics at National Taiwan University. In 2022 she was elected to the Academia Sinica. She is married to Chen Kuei-hsien, a physicist who works for Academia Sinica's Institute of Atomic and Molecular Sciences.
