Sergio Bertolucci (born 1 January 1950) is an Italian particle physicist, and a former Director of Research and Scientific Computing at CERN (Conseil Européen pour la Recherche Nucléaire). He was born in La Spezia. He studied Particle Physics at the University of Pisa. He did further research at DESY in Germany. From 2002-04 he worked at the Laboratori Nazionali di Frascati (LNF), where he was Director. From 2005-08 he worked at Istituto Nazionale di Fisica Nucleare (INFN) in Italy. He served as CERN Director of Research from 2009 to 2015.
