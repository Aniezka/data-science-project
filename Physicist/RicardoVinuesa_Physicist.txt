Ricardo Vinuesa Motilva (born February 4, 1986, in Valencia, Spain) is a fluid dynamicist and machine-learning researcher. He is an associate professor at KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm (Sweden), as well as an Affiliated Researcher at the AI Sustainability Center. He is also vice director of the KTH Digitalization Platform. He is known for his work on turbulence (including computational fluid dynamics and machine-learning methods) and sustainability. Vinuesa led an influential study on the impact of AI on the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) of the United Nations (UN).
