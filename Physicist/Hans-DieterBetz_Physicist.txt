Hans-Dieter Betz (born 29 September 1940) is a German professor emeritus of experimental physics. Beside atomic physics Betz searched on Sferics, where he leads a science-group on the Munich Ludwig-Maximilians-University. Betz also investigates Radiesthesia and Dowsing, ten years in Order by the German Government. For example the extensive Munich Scheunenexperimenten.
