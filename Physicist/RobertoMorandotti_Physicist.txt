Roberto Morandotti is a physicist and full Professor, working in the Energy Materials Telecommunications Department of the Institut National de la Recherche Scientifique (INRS-EMT, Montreal, Canada). The work of his team includes the areas of integrated and quantum photonics, nonlinear and singular optics, as well as terahertz photonics. 1993: M.Sc. Physics, University of Genoa1999: Ph.D. Electrical Engineering, University of Glasgow 1999-2001: Postdoctoral Researcher, Weizmann Institute of Science2001-2002: Postdoctoral Researcher, University of Toronto2003-2008: Associate Professor, INRS-EMT, Université du Québec2008 - : Full Professor, INRS-EMT, Université du Québec
