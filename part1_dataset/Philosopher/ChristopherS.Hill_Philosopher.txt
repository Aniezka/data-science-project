Christopher S. Hill (born 1942) is an American philosopher and William Herbert Perry Faunce Professor of Philosophy at Brown University.He is known for his expertise on consciousness and philosophy of mind. Hill previously taught at the University of Pittsburgh, Case Western Reserve University, the University of Michigan, the University of Arkansas, and Massachusetts Institute of Technology.He has held various fellowships from the National Endowment for the Humanities and is a fellow at the National Humanities Center.
