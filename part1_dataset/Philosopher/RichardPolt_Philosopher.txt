Richard F. H. Polt is a professor of philosophy at Xavier University in Cincinnati, Ohio. He has written about and translated works by Martin Heidegger. Polt is a typewriter enthusiast active on the Typosphere[unreliable source?] and a former editor of the quarterly ETCetera publication about manual typewriters.
