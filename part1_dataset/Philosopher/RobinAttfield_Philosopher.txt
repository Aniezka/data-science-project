Robin Attfield, MA (Oxon), PhD (Wales) is a Professor of Philosophy at Cardiff University since 1992. Robin Attfield read Greats (Literae Humaniores) at Christ Church and theology at Regent's Park College, Oxford. He is a member of the Council of the Royal Institute of Philosophy; a co-opted member of the executive committee of the British Philosophical Association; Chair of the Cardiff Branch of the Royal Institute of Philosophy and a member of the UNESCO working party on environmental ethics.
