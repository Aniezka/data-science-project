Arindam Chakrabarti is, currently, a visiting professor of philosophy at Ashoka University, India. He is, also, a professor of philosophy at Stony Brook University, where he has been since 2018. Prior to moving to Stony Brook, Chakrabarti taught at the University of Hawaii, where he was the director of the EPOCH Project (Eastern Philosophy of Consciousness and the Humanities). Chakrabarti obtained his BA in 1976 and his MA in 1978, both from the University of Calcutta. He received his Ph.D. in 1982 from St. Anne's College at Oxford University.
