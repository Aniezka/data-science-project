Hubert Schleichert (born July 30, 1935 in Vienna - September 13, 2020 in Munich) was an Austrian emeritus philosopher (from Vienna), now living in Konstanz. His works have emphasized political philosophy, theory of argument and non-European philosophy, especially Chinese philosophy. Working with Urs Egli, Schleichert contributed to erotetics (theory of questions) by assembling an annotated bibliography of works in philosophy, linguistics, automatic answering, and psychology/pedagogy.
