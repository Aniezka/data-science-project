Chih-Kang Shih (aka Ken Shih) is a physicist, currently the Dr. Arnold Romberg Endowed Chair and Jane and Roland Blumberg Professor at University of Texas at Austin. In 1988, Shih earned a PhD from Stanford University. In 1982, Shih's technical career began as a research assistant at Stanford Electronics Lab at Stanford University. Shih is a Professor of Physics at University of Texas at Austin.
