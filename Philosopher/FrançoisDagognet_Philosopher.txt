François Dagognet (French: [dagɔɲɛ]; 24 April 1924 – 3 October 2015) was a 20th-century French philosopher. François Dagognet was born in Langres. He studied both science and philosophy, and was a student of Georges Canguilhem. He was Professor of Philosophy at the University of Lyon before becoming Professor of Philosophy at Pantheon-Sorbonne University. Dagognet wrote extensively on the philosophy of the body.
