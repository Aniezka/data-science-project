Eduardo Nicol (Barcelona, Catalonia 13 December 1907 - México 6 May 1990) was a Mexican-Catalan philosopher. He arrived in Mexico in 1939, obtained his major in philosophy from National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM), the biggest university in Mexico, where he taught from 1940. While at UNAM, he became the chairs of adolescent psychology and the history of psychology following Ezequiel A. Chávez [es].
