Rabinowicz's areas of expertise include ethics, normativity, decision theory and utilitarianism. From 1995 to 1998, he was editor-in-chief of Theoria. He began his undergraduate studies at the University of Warsaw, but after the 1968 Polish political crisis he moved to the University of Uppsala, where he graduated in 1970. He received his doctorate in 1979.
