Rebecca Suzanne Whisnant is professor and chair of the philosophy department at the University of Dayton. Whisnant gained her degree in philosophy from Oberlin College in 1989. She went on to complete a masters (1992), and a Ph.D. (2002), both in philosophy, at University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. Prior to working at the University of Dayton, Whisnant taught at the University of Southern Indiana. Book review
