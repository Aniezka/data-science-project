Philipp W. Rosemann (born February 24, 1964, in Frankfurt) is a German philosopher and Cottrill-Rolfes Chair at University of Kentucky. Previously he was Professor of Philosophy at Maynooth University. He is the co-editor of Dallas Medieval Texts and Translations. Prior to his tenure at Maynooth, he taught at the University of Dallas for twenty years. He is a member of the Royal Irish Academy and of the ecumenical Beatrice Institute of Pittsburgh.
