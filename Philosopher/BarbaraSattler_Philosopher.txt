Barbara Michaela Sattler (born 1974) is a Senior Lecturer in philosophy at St Andrews University. Her area of research is metaphysics and natural philosophy in the ancient Greek world. From January to March 2017, she was an Institute of Advanced Study Fellow at St John's College, Durham. Sattler was assistant professor at Yale University from 2007 to 2013, where she was a Fellow of the Whitney Humanities Center.
