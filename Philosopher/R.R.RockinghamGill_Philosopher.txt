Richard Rowan Rockingham Gill (born 1944, South Africa) was a lecturer of philosophy—in particular, logic—and is an author. Rockingham Gill received a MA from the University of St Andrews and has published variously in the Journal of Symbolic Logic, Archiv für mathematische Logik und Grundlagenforschung (Archive for Mathematical Logic and Basic Research) and other journals. He lectured at the University of Wales, Lampeter and is also the former head of the Department of Philosophy.
